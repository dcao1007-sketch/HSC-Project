# Business plan

## Positioning

Arc is the focused past-paper practice workspace for NSW Mathematics, not a general homework chatbot. Its advantage is a structured catalogue, question-level revision loop and syllabus-linked evidence of progress.

## Customer and channel hypotheses

- Students and parents: freemium direct subscription after a useful free paper set
- Tutors: small-team dashboard and assigned paper sets
- Schools: annual licence, class insights and approved content library
- Acquisition: student referrals, tutor partnerships, exam-season content and school pilots

## Revenue experiments

Test willingness to pay before building billing: interview 15 students, 10 parents and 5 tutors; pilot a free tier plus an optional premium paper library; measure conversion intent and retention. Any school product requires stronger privacy controls and teacher administration first.

## Near-term risks

Content permissions, PDF quality, seasonal usage, answer reliability and an insufficient paper catalogue are the main business risks. Validate content rights and repeated student use before paid acquisition.
