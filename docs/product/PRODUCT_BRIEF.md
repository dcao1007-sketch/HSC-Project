# Product brief

Arc helps NSW Mathematics students turn past-paper practice into a repeatable loop: attempt, self-mark, identify mistakes, revisit the exact question and solution, then measure improvement by syllabus area.

## Primary users

Year 11–12 Mathematics Advanced, Extension 1 and Extension 2 students. Year 9–10 and Standard courses are later expansion paths.

## Current release scope

- One complete Abbotsleigh 2025 Extension 1 paper and solution
- Visible timed attempt with solutions locked until completion
- Part-level self-marking with maximum-mark validation
- Empty-by-default personal bank populated only by explicit Redo choices
- Original PDF question and solution pages in review cards
- Average mark, average completion time and NESA-area performance

## Success measures

Track weekly active students, completed papers per active student, percentage of banked questions reviewed, return rate after seven days, and improvement on repeated syllabus areas. Do not optimise for time-on-app alone.
