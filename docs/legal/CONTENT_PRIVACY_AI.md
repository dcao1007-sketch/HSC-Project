# Content, privacy and AI safeguards

Obtain and record publication rights for every school paper and solution before a public launch. A source being publicly downloadable does not automatically grant republishing rights. Keep source attribution and a takedown contact.

Collect the minimum student data. Learning goals and results can be sensitive educational information; publish a clear privacy notice, provide deletion/export, avoid advertising profiles, encrypt account data and apply age-appropriate consent controls.

AI explanations must be grounded in the selected question and approved solution, distinguish source material from generated guidance, avoid presenting uncertain working as fact, and offer a reporting path. Do not send identifiable student data to an AI provider without disclosure and an appropriate agreement.

This file is a product risk checklist, not legal advice; obtain Australian legal review before launch.
